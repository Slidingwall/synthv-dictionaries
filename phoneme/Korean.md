# Korean 韩语

{: .new-title }
> List of Abbreviations / 简称列表
>
> `KOR` = Korean X-SAMPA  
> `IPA` = International Phonetic Alphabet  
> `MAN` = Mandarin X-SAMPA  
> `ARP` = English Arpabet & Arpasing  
> `RMJ` = Japanese Romaji
